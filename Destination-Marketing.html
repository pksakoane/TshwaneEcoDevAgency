DESTINATION MARKETING
Destination Marketing

Tourism promotion is the newest addition to the business portfolio of the Tshwane Economic Development Agency (TEDA). Our mandate expanded in 2019 to include tourism, a function that was migrated from the City of Tshwane and falls within TEDA’s Destination Marketing Division.

Tshwane has a unique blend of tourist attractions that include heritage sites, biodiversity and cultural and sport attractions that appeal to a wide range of tourists. There are also many conference and hospitality facilities in Tshwane, and it has the capacity to host events. All of this is supported by a growing public transport infrastructure that includes the Gautrain, the A Re Yeng rapid transit system and Tshwane Bus Services.

Tshwane is South Africa’s seat of government. It is an African knowledge capital with a high concentration of innovation, academic and research institutions. It is also a diplomatic capital that houses more than 130 embassies and foreign missions. This unique combination of attributes provides a compelling reason to visit and do business in the capital city of South Africa.

The Destination Marketing Division promotes Tshwane as a preferred destination for business, tourism and events to domestic, regional and international markets in order to increase economic growth in Tshwane.

The Destination Marketing Division has the following operational units:
• Business Tourism • Visitor Services • Trade Relations
