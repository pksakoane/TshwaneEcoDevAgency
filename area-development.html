Area Based Development
Area Based Development

The area based development focus areas will be to define the desired development direction of the nodal interventions; precinct plan development; precinct development packing; and spatial based interventions. It is envisaged for the projects to be an ongoing process of local area elaboration and planning complementing and supporting the Metropolitan and Regional Spatial Development Frameworks of the City of Tshwane.
